IEEE-Day

EEE Day, the day that marked the new beginning to change the world through technology and community work was first acknowledged in 1884 . It is affiliated to no federation in particular but to all thereby satisfying its nature of a global community. While the world benefits from what’s new, IEEE focuses on what’s next. IEEE Day is moreover a celebration that successive generations of IEEE members and volunteers have been doing for more than a century.This year we celebrate the 12th annual IEEE Day with the theme “Leveraging Technology for a Better Tomorrow" on 5th October . Although IEEE Day is lauded on First Tuesday in October , the gala of events remain active for many days and this year in between 25th September and 17th October. We as IEEE representatives hallmark this day to pass the significance of IEEE and its objectives. We continue to build on the number of activities held around the world, and expand the local opportunities for IEEE members and colleagues to collaborate.

Theme

The global technical day that is IEEE Day is set for its commemoration on 17th October this year in our GVPCE under the supervision of IEEE Student Branch GVPCE(A) . On this occasion we are set to explore  the essence of the theme  "Upskilling , Going above and beyond". We are ready  to spirit everyone up in believing that they can  make a difference to their communities by skilling up and investing it for a brighter future. It also encourages IEEE members  to share ideas and be inspired to develop  technology for a better today and tomorrow  ."Upskilling ,Going above and beyond '' is not just a theme for this year’s IEEE Day. It is a call to action  to actively engage and take up initiatives to explore today’s emerging technologies.Our passion, commitment,  desire to envision, innovate, and create are the hallmarks that define our  efforts and inspire others to join in . All the series of events that we planned will be intriguing and pleasurable. They range from technical webinars to innovative art competitions to ice breaking fun activities.  So, keep a note in your calendar and join us to experience diversity which is surely worth your time.
